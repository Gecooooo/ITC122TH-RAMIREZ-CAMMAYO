VISION

The Alumni Services and Placement Office (ASPO) shall be an engaging and mutually beneficial lifetime link between Arellano University and its community of alumni.

Guiding Statements

The Alumni Services and Placement Office (ASPO) shall be responsible for the administration of key programs for students and alumni services of the university. Its priority concerns shall be focused on providing programs and facilities for student/alumni services; providing current and relevant information about the organizations of the various alumni groups of the Arellano University; and coordination of a range services in support of university initiatives for alumni welfare, as well as independent activities of AU alumni groups and their respective organizations.
